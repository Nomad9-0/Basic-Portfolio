About Me


I am Oscar Jimenez. I graduated with a degree in Business Administration/Business Law and was intending to become a lawyer. I worked as an analyst for medical device companies before discovering my passion for web-development. I was introduced to programming in my previous rolls and realized the critial thinking and problem solving appealed to me.

I began teaching myself web-developing skills in late 2016 and began taking on freelancing jobs in late 2017. My constant drive to learn new skills fit perfectly with programming. I love learning new skills and perfecting my craft. Programming to me is the perfect career because of the continuous learning involved. It is such a fullfilling career.

I decided to join the UCLA Coding Bootcamp to expand my skills and get help with the backend portion of web-development. I look forward to learning new skills and joining a great company where I can not only apply my skills bit also continue my learning.
